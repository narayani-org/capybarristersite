Cookie Policy

Understand how we use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Capybarrister uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us provide you with a better experience by enabling certain features and functionality.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

  • Authentication cookies
  • Security cookies
  • Session management

3.2 Functional Cookies

  • Language preferences
  • User interface customization
  • Remembered settings

3.3 Analytics Cookies

  • Usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error tracking

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use some third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics
  • Payment processors
  • Customer support tools

5. Cookie Duration

5.1 Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.

5.2 Persistent Cookies

Cookies that remain on your device for a set period of time.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our platform.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through our Platform or via email.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at privacy@capybarrister.com.
